Hangman

The rules are simple: Player 1 types in a word. Player 2 will guess that word. You have 5 lives. An incorrect guess is a life. Get to 0, and you lose!

Guess a letter

Hangman is a classic game, in which a player must guess a secret word, lest his 'hangman' be hanged for failing. The origin of the game is unclear, though it appears as early as 1894, under the name "Birds, Beasts, and Fishes".
